thâtiy Dârayavauš xšâyathiya pasâva Naditabaira hadâ kamnaibiš asabâraibiš amutha Bâbirum ašiyava pasâva adam Bâbirum ašiyavam vašnâ Auramazdâha utâ Bâbirum agarbâyam utâ avam Naditabairam agarbâyam pasâva avam Naditabairam adam Bâbirauv avâjanam thâtiy Dârayavauš xšâyathiya yâtâ adam Babirauv âham imâ dahyâva tyâ hacâma hamiçiyâ abava Pârsa Ûvja Mâda Athurâ Mudrâya Parthava Marguš Thataguš Saka thâtiy Dârayavauš xšâyathiya I martiya Martiya nâ<sep>King Darius says: Then did Nidintu-Bêl with a few horsemen into Babylon. Thereupon I marched to Babylon. By the grace of Ahuramazda I took Babylon, and captured Nidintu-Bêl. Then I slew that Nidintu-Bêl in Babylon.King Darius says: While I was in Babylon, these provinces revolted from me: Persia, Elam, Media, Assyria, Egypt, Parthia, Margiana, Sattagydia, and Scythia.
